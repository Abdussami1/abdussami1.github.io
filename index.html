About SnapDrive

SnapDrive is an Android application that allows users to capture photos, record videos, and record audio, and securely upload them directly to their personal Google Drive account.

SnapDrive does not store your data on any external servers. All uploads are performed securely using Google’s official authentication and APIs.

Key Features

  • Capture photos and upload to Google Drive
  • Record videos and store them securely
  • Record audio and back it up instantly
  • Uses your own Google account and storage
